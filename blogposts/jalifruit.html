Jali Fruit: A Snack that's positive and delicious

Empowering women & eliminating food waste

June 14, 2020

Hey guys!

So today I’m sharing with you Jali Fruit Co., which is a tree-to-table, food waste reducing, and women empowering brand that brings direct-trade dried fruit right to you!

Jali focuses on empowering women farmers across the Carribean and Africa by providing a way for them to market their dried fruit products on a global scale.

The women are provided 7x higher daily wage than the average local farmer and they are also given transferable skills on agriculture, market practices, and financial literacy for future employment.

They have three delicious fruit products:

✧ Mango-rama
✧ Mr. Jackfruit
✧ Party Pineapple

Every year there is approximately 2.8 trillion pounds of food waste. In Sub-Saharan Africa, 95% of food waste occurs before it ever reaches a market. Through direct-trade, Jali offers high-quality fruit products without the middle-man to ensure they are ethically-sourced.

In fact, every bag of Jali Fruit has a QR code that you can scan to find exactly where your fruit came from and the women who lead that cooperative!
