Staycation at Conrad DC

Our stay at the Sakura Club in Conrad Hotel DC!

December 28, 2020

We took a staycation!! Now, I know I haven’t updated the blog recently but it’s honestly because I’ve been spending a majority of my time on the Youtube channel (check it out linked up top if you haven’t!) but I’m back.

The views from our sakura suite!

We had a chance to stay at Conrad DC, a stunning hotel featuring the Sakura Club, an exclusive lounging area with a private chef and amazing amenities. Our room had floor to ceiling windows, an espresso machine, a waterfall shower, and everything you need to enjoy a relaxing weekend getaway!

Loved all the amentities that came with the room!
The Sakura lounge serves a wide variety of drinks for all tastes.

My favorite part was the food, I swear everything that came out of the kitchen was pure deliciousness! The service was also topnotch, everyone was so welcoming and friendly, the rooms and lounge areas were sparkling clean and I felt very safe.

They serve breakfast & dinner as part of the Sakura suite package.
The berry parfait was one of my favorites from breakfast!

Of course, we are still in the midst of a pandemic and that’s something we always kept in mind during our mini weekend staycation. Masks must be worn in all public areas of the hotel and everything was sanitized multiple times per day. There’s also an outdoor lounge area with fire towers to stay warm that recently re-opened again for the holiday weekends.

Their lounge is the perfect place to relax or even catch up on work, up to you!
Christmas decor was up when we visited, loved the aesthetic of the hotel.

I definitely recommend booking a Sakura Club room if you’re ever in the DC area or visiting Conrad Hotel, it’s the perfect pick-me-up to unwind from the stress of 2020. I’ll be sharing more whenever travel restrictions are lifted in the states and I’m excited to hit the roads (or flights) again in the new year! Happy new year and cheers to 2021!

Will definitely plan on coming back in 2021!
